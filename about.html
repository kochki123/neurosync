About

A concept built around human possibility

NeuroSync Suit is a forward-looking design exploration in assistive neurotechnology.

NeuroSync Suit is a conceptual innovation project imagining what assistive technology can become when AI, soft robotics, and neuroscience converge into a single, wearable system.

It is designed for people living with partial or full paralysis — and for the clinicians, families, and engineers working to give them back their independence.

Every choice in the system, from modularity to comfort to expandability, starts with one question: what would help most, today and ten years from now?

Project at a glance

  • Status: Conceptual design
  • Focus: Assistive neurotechnology
  • Audience: Patients, clinicians, researchers
  • Roadmap: Modular & expandable

Note: NeuroSync Suit is a conceptual project. The descriptions, performance figures, and modules shown here illustrate a long-term design vision rather than a currently shipping medical product.
