Where We Are Going

A future where no one is left immobile

NeuroSync is built to grow — from a single glove today to full-body neuro-integration tomorrow.

Restoring movement is just the beginning. We are designing the bridge between human intention and the world.

Phase 01

Single-limb assistance

Glove and sleeve modules for hand and arm rehabilitation in clinical settings.

Phase 02

Mobility & gait

NeuroSync Boots and integrated lower-body suit for at-home walking support.

Phase 03

Full-body integration

Coordinated upper- and lower-body modules with shared intent prediction.

Phase 04

Neural co-pilot

Adaptive AI that anticipates daily routines, removing friction from every action.

Phase 05

Human-machine fluency

Open neuro-interfaces extending NeuroSync beyond mobility — independence, expression, connection.
