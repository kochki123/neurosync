Under the Hood

The Technology

Four layers, working as one — sensing, thinking, moving, and learning.

📈

Layer 01EMG Sensors

High-density electrode arrays read electrical activity directly from your muscles — even faint signals are enough.

🧠

Layer 02Optional EEG Interface

A non-invasive headband adds brain-signal context for users with limited muscle activity.

🤖

Layer 03AI Processing

An on-device neural network fuses signals, predicts intent, and decides exactly how to assist — all in under 12ms.

🦾

Layer 04Soft Robotics

Lightweight pneumatic and tendon-driven actuators move with you — never against you.
